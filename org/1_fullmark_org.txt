This is a case description about an organized threat actor group. The group is known as Disco Team, also referred to as Equipo del Discoteca. It is a professional criminal organization that engages in various criminal activities for profit. The primary goal of this group is to steal credit card information. Its members are experts and have substantial resources, reaching an organization-level resource capacity. The main motivation for Disco Team is financial gain. The group uses the email disco-team@stealthemail.com as contact information.
